Who We Are

Building a culture of lifelong learning

At the GEC Kozhikode chapter of the Mulearn Foundation, we empower students and foster a culture of continuous growth. Founded on the belief that education drives transformation, we connect students across disciplines to explore technology, entrepreneurship, and leadership.

Our chapter provides accessible skill development, innovative workshops, and a vibrant community for future leaders — all under one roof. Join us as we learn, grow, and shape a brighter future together.
